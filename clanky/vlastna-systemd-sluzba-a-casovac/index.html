Vlastna Systemd sluzba a casovac

Jan 1, 0001

Type=oneshot → Runs the command and does not restart after exit. RemainAfterExit=yes → Marks the service as active even after the script finishes.

[Service] ExecStart=/usr/local/bin/setup.sh Type=oneshot RemainAfterExit=yes

[Install] WantedBy=multi-user.target

Type=simple → Assumes the process runs indefinitely in the foreground. Restart=always → If the process crashes, systemd will restart it.

[Service] ExecStart=/usr/bin/python3 -m http.server 8080 Restart=always User=www-data WorkingDirectory=/var/www

[Install] WantedBy=multi-user.target

[Timer] OnCalendar=--* 02:00:00 Unit=dailybackup.service

[Install] WantedBy=timers.target
