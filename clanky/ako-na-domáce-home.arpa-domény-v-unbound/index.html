Ako na domáce home.arpa domény v Unbound

27. February 2026 • Dodoslav
dns unbound arpa home.arpa local
Ako si vytvoriť a zprovozniť domácu DNS zónu home.arpa pre domáce použitie.

Vytvorenie zóny:

echo 'server:
local-zone: "home.arpa." nodefault

auth-zone:
name: "home.arpa."
zonefile: "/etc/unbound/unbound.conf.d/home-arpa.zone"
for-downstream: yes
for-upstream: no
' > /etc/unbound/unbound.conf.d/home-arpa.conf

Vytvorenie záznamov:

echo '$TTL 30
$ORIGIN home.arpa.

@ IN SOA ns1.home.arpa. hostmaster.home.arpa. (
2026022701 ; serial (YYYYMMDDnn)
3600 ; refresh
900 ; retry
1209600 ; expire
300 ) ; negative cache

IN NS ns1.home.arpa.

# adresa resolvera
ns1 IN A 192.168.0.2

; --- Your requested records ---
router IN A 192.168.0.1
' > /etc/unbound/unbound.conf.d/home-arpa.zone
